Privacy Policy for PokeQuiz

Last updated: January 2025

1. Introduction

PokeQuiz ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

PokeQuiz is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal information:

  • Game Progress: Your coins, streak, and power-ups are stored locally on your device using SharedPreferences.
  • Language Preference: Your selected language is stored locally.
  • Network Access: We connect to the public PokéAPI (https://pokeapi.co) to fetch Pokémon data for quiz questions.

We do NOT collect:

  • Personal identifiable information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Analytics or tracking data

3. Third-Party Services

PokeQuiz uses the following third-party services:

3.1 PokéAPI

We use the public PokéAPI (https://pokeapi.co) to fetch Pokémon information. PokéAPI does not require authentication and does not track users. For more information, see PokéAPI's website.

3.2 AppLovin (Advertising)

PokeQuiz uses AppLovin SDK to display advertisements. AppLovin may collect certain information for ad personalization and delivery. For more details, please review AppLovin's Privacy Policy.

4. Data Storage

All game data (coins, streak, inventory) is stored locally on your device using Android's SharedPreferences. This data is never transmitted to our servers or any third party, except as described in the Third-Party Services section.

5. Children's Privacy

PokeQuiz is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Since we do not collect personal data, parents can safely allow their children to use the app.

6. Data Security

Since we do not collect personal information, there is minimal risk to your privacy. Game progress is stored securely on your device using Android's standard storage mechanisms.

7. Your Rights

You can delete all game data at any time by clearing the app's data in your device settings or by uninstalling the app.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
